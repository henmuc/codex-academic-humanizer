# Chinese Academic Style

## Goals

Make Chinese scholarly writing natural, precise, and authorial while preserving formal academic tone. Prefer clear logical progression over generic "AI-polished" smoothness.

## Common AI-Like Patterns to Reduce

- Stock openings: "随着...的发展", "近年来...受到广泛关注", "本文旨在", "具有重要意义".
- Empty evaluation: "显著提升", "有效解决", "充分证明", "具有较强的鲁棒性" when unsupported by the source.
- Mechanical connectors: repeated "首先/其次/最后", "此外/与此同时/进一步地" without real logical progression.
- Overpacked noun phrases: long strings of abstract nouns that obscure the actor, method, or result.
- Repetitive paragraph endings: "为...提供了参考", "奠定了基础", "具有一定借鉴意义".

## Revision Preferences

- Replace broad background claims with context already present in the source.
- Prefer concrete verbs: "表明", "显示", "刻画", "约束", "解释", "验证", "比较", "构建".
- Keep necessary academic caution: "可能", "在...条件下", "一定程度上", "初步表明", "与...一致".
- Avoid unnecessary synonym substitution for fixed terms. Use the same term consistently.
- Vary sentence length, but keep logic explicit. Split sentences when a chain of clauses hides the main claim.
- Preserve citation placement and citation scope. Do not move a citation so it appears to support a different claim.

## Section-Specific Guidance

- Abstract: keep problem, method, result, and contribution compact. Do not add background flourish.
- Introduction: make the gap and motivation specific. Avoid generic importance claims.
- Methods: prioritize procedural clarity and reproducibility. Do not embellish.
- Results: separate observation from interpretation. Preserve numerical values and comparison baselines exactly.
- Discussion: keep causal language cautious unless the original establishes causality.
- Conclusion: summarize demonstrated findings, not desired implications.

## Preferred Transformations

- "本文旨在探究..." -> "本文考察..." or "本文围绕...分析..."
- "具有重要理论和实践意义" -> specify the actual contribution if present; otherwise omit or soften.
- "实验结果充分证明..." -> "实验结果表明..." unless the source justifies a stronger claim.
- "进一步验证了方法的有效性" -> "结果显示，该方法在...指标上..." when source details are available.
